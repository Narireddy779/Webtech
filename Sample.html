About BYD

Founded in February 1994, BYD is a high-tech company devoted to leveraging technological innovations for a better life. After more than 30 years of rapid growth, BYD has played a significant role in industries related to electronics, auto, renewable energy and rail transit. With a focus on energy acquisition, storage, and application, BYD offers comprehensive zero-emission new energy solutions. As a company listed on both the Hong Kong Stock Exchange and Shenzhen Stock Exchange, its annual revenue in 2023 exceeds RMB 602 billion.

BYD Yangwang U9

Highlighted Features BYD Yangwang U9 is an all-electric sports car designed by the world's biggest EV maker. BYD U9 is based on the next generation e4 platform, which allows BYD to implement quad-motor powertrains into its electric vehicles. U9's performance will be on par with modern super cars. It offers an acceleration performance from 0 to 100 km/h (62 mph) in around two seconds! BYD Yangwang U9 will cost more than 1 million CNY, approx. $148,000 in China. Yangwang-branded vehicles will enter production later in the 2023.
